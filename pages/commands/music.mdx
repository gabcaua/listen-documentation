# Music Commands

As an audio player bot, Listen has the following commands:

<Callout type="warning" emoji="">
  These commands require the user to be connected to a voice channel.
</Callout>

The `/play` command plays an audio or playlist.

**Here you can be the DJ.**\
To control the basics of the bot, use the buttons available in the DJ Control Panel.

#### Liking a song

To like a song, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Thumbs Up</span>** button.

#### Marking a song as your Current Obsession

To mark a song as your Current Obsession, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Loop Symbol</span>** button.

#### Live Lyrics

To see synchronized lyrics, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Synced Lyrics</span>** button, highlighted in green <u>when available</u>.

#### Shuffle

To shuffle the queue, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Shuffle Symbol</span>** button.

#### Replaying the previous song

To replay the previous song, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Rewind</span>** button.

#### Pausing or resuming playback

To pause the current song, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Pause</span>** button.

#### Skipping to the next song

To vote to skip to the next song, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Skip</span>** button.

#### Disconnecting

To disconnect the bot, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Stop playing</span>** button.

#### Queue

To get an overview of the queue, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Queue Symbol</span>** button.

#### Removing items from the queue
In order to control the queue and remove items, use the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>`/queue`</span>** command.

#### See legal disclaimer

You can find it in the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Legal Disclaimer</span>** button and in the top section of this page.

#### Loop

To alter between repeat modes, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>`Loop`</span>** button.

#### Going to page two

To go to page two, press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>See more</span>** button.

#### Filters

Press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Audio Effects</span>** button.

#### Sharing a song

Press the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Share with Shareables</span>** button.

#### Seek

Press one of the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Seek</span>** buttons, indicated by symbols.

#### Volume Control

Press one of the **<span style={{color: "green"}}>Volume</span>** buttons, also indicated by symbols.
