# Language Setup

<Callout type="info">
  Due to lack of financial and community support, Listen uses **AI** to translate each piece of code.\
  To maintain Listen's AI working, we need **[donations of 5$](https://patreon.com/iamcaua)** to buy in OpenAI credits monthly.
</Callout>

### Changing the language

<Steps>
    ### Go to the <span style={{color:"teal"}}>Language Menu</span>.
    There are three ways to access this menu:
    <Tabs items={['Mention', 'Natural Language', 'Slash Command']}>
        <Tabs.Tab>
            <Callout type="info">
            When you mention **Listen** in a text channel, click on Languages to open the menu.

            ![](/language/mention.png)
            </Callout>
        </Tabs.Tab>
        <Tabs.Tab>
            <Callout type="info">
            You can ask Listen to show you the available languages by sending **Listen languages** in a text channel\
            To keep Listen's understanding of human, natural language, consider [donating](https://patreon.com/iamcaua).

            ![](/language/natural-language.png)
            </Callout>
        </Tabs.Tab>
        <Tabs.Tab>
            <Callout type="info">
            Simply use the slash command **`/listen` `language`** to open the menu.

            <img src="/language/slash.png" style={{borderRadius: "12px"}}/>
            </Callout>
        </Tabs.Tab>
    </Tabs>
    ### Select the language you want to use
    You have over 25+ languages available. Select what best suits your community!
    ### Enjoy the perks of choosing Listen
    Listen is a bot that is free to use, and we want to give you the best experience possible. 
</Steps>
